History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local developers and designers. It quickly grew into a major international conference, recognized for its commitment to open source and emerging technologies. Our first event featured 50 attendees; last year, we hosted over 5,000 guests from 40 different countries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, facilitate knowledge sharing, and connect the world's most creative minds. We believe in breaking down barriers to technology and making advanced concepts accessible to everyone, regardless of background or experience level. Our core principles are Inclusivity, Innovation, and Impact.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Anya Sharma (2022)

Dr. Sharma is a renowned expert in ethical AI and spoke about the social responsibilities of developers. Her work has fundamentally changed how we approach data privacy in machine learning.

Kenji Tanaka (2021)

Kenji Tanaka, founder of a major cloud infrastructure company, gave a keynote on the future of serverless computing. His insights guided many startups toward scalable architecture.
